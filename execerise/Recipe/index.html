Crab Rangoon Toast

Prep time: 30 Minutes

Cook Time: 15 Minutes

Total Time: 45 Minutes

Yield: 6

If you love crab rangoon and crispy bread, you’re in for a treat with these Crab Rangoon Sandwiches! This delightful fusion takes inspiration from Chinese shrimp toast, combining the creamy, savory filling you adore with the satisfying crunch of toasted bread. It’s the perfect appetizer or snack that will impress your friends and family!

Ingredients

Crab filling

  • 1 pound crab imitation meat
  • 1/2 cup kewpie mayo
  • 1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 egg white
  • 1/3 cup cream cheese, room temp
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

Other

  • 12 slices stale white bread
  • vegetable oil, for frying
  • sweet chili sauce, for dipping

1 a large bowl, add the imitation crab meat, Kewpie mayo, soy sauce, egg white, and cream cheese. Mix until everything is well combined.

2 the garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and chopped green onions. Stir to incorporate.

3 Sprinkle in the cornstarch and gently fold it into the mixture to help bind the filling together. Set the crab filling aside.

4 Spread a generous amount of crab filling on one slice of bread in each pair. Top with a second slice of bread to create a sandwich.

5 You can leave the bread as whole slices or cut each slice in half for smaller sandwiches, depending on your preference.

6 Spread a generous amount of crab filling on one slice of bread in each pair. Top with a second slice of bread to create a sandwich.

7 Press down lightly on the sandwich to ensure the filling adheres well.

8 Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or deep pan over medium heat, ensuring the oil is deep enough for shallow frying (about 1/4 to 1/2 inch).

9 Once the oil is hot, carefully place the sandwiches in the pan. Fry for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden and crispy.

10 Flip the sandwiches and fry the other side for another 2-3 minutes until it’s golden and crispy.

11 Flip the sandwiches and fry the other side for another 2-3 minutes until it’s golden and crispy.

12 Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauces, such as sweet chili or soy sauce.
